About me

Hey there, I'm Demi!

Im the passionate photographer behind Photography by Demi. As a mother myself, I've experienced the incredible journey of parenthood and understand just how quickly time passed. The inspiration for my photography business stemmed from the desire to help families and expecting parents capture the fleeting moments of this magical chapter in their lives.

The essence of my work is to help you relive the love, anticipation, and joy you felt during this time. My portfolio showcases the unique connection between families and the tenderness of pregnancy.

From wardrobe selection to expert advice on locations, I'm here to guide you through every step of the process. Your maternity, newborn, and family photos are an investment in lasting memories, and I'm honored to be a part of your journey. Togeather, let's celebrate the beauty of life, love, and the unique moments that define your family's story.
